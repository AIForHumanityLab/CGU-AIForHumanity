About The Lab

The AI for Humanity Lab is a research, teaching, and innovation space. Through research collaborations, student-led initiatives, and industry partnerships, we explore how AI can serve society in transparent, fair, and accountable ways.

Our Pillars

Human-Centered Design

AI that empowers rather than replaces.

Ethics at Every Stage

Development with safeguards, responsibility, and equity.

Education

AI literacy for students, educators, and decision-makers.

Research With Impact

Evidence-based policy, governance, and social innovation.

Projects & Initiatives

AI for Humanity Compass

A decision-support tool guiding AI projects to align with human dignity, safety, and well-being.

CGU Hacks

A Transdisciplinary hackathon bringing together students, researchers and practitioners to design responsible AI solutions addressing real-world social and ethical challenges.
